About Me

Hello, I'm Andrew! I am a full-stack web developer with an interest in software engineering. I love creating engaging designs and adding capabilities to existing projects. Keep reading and see what I've made, who I am, and how I can bring your project to life.

More About Me

While on active duty, I was a Coast Guard attorney advising in everything from military justice to signals intelligence in the some to the busiest law offices in the fleet. As I prepare to separate, I am seeking new challenges and want to start building engaging experiences that can scale to everyone in the world. I bring with me commitment to lifelong learning, service, discipline, and leadership that the Coast Guard instilled in me.'

On the front end, I use with HTML5, CSS3 with Flexbox, Javascript ES6 and React.JS. On the back end, I use Node.JS, C#, MongoDB, Express, and Jest for testing. Technologies I use include Visual Studio, Git/GitHub, Bash, npm, and more. Let's build something toghether!

I currently serve the Severn, Maryland area, and am always looking to connect to my community. If you are community service organization, consider reaching out with the contact form below and let's see if I can build something for you.
